Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie Recipe

Description

I mean, who doesn't like a wholesome smoothie, no matter the flavor? Let's make a lovely peanut butter banana smoothie from scratch!

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 0.5 cups of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth (about 30 seconds)
  4. Enjoy!
