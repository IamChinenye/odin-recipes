A Recipe for Pancakes

Description

You need not be a chef to make some awesome pancakes. You just need this basic pancake recipe and a little bit of love and you can serve your way into a world of tasty deliciousness.

Still uncertain? Let's take a look at what you need...

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1.25 cups of milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Sift together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Whisk in milk, melted butter, and egg until combined. Let batter rest for 5 minutes. Preheat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Spray with cooking spray.
  3. Pour batter into the hot skillet, about 0.25 cups of batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles appear on the sides annd center of each pancake, about two to three minutes.
  4. Flip and cook until the bottom is golden, about one to two minutes.
  5. Serve with butter and maple syrup and enjoy.
