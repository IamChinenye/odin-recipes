A Recipe for Sweet Potatoes

Description

Hey, I know this aint your usual chips or fries, but how about we switch it up a bit?
Let's prepare some of the best potatoes you will ever taste.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 5 potatoes, quartered

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. In a small bowl, mix mayonnaise, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until it's almost done (about ten minutes). Do not overcook to prevent the potatoes from breaking apart. Drain and cool.
  4. Preheat oven broiler. Line a baking tray with aluminium foil, and lightly grease the aluminium foil.
  5. Arrange potatoes in the prepared baking tray. Spoon the mayonnaise mixture over the potatoes.
  6. Broil in the preheated oven until the potatoes are tender and mayonnaise mixture is lightly browned (about ten minutes).
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
